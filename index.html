Designed for the future

Introducing and extensible editor

Blogr features an exceedingly intuitive interface which lets you focus on one thing: creating content. The editor supports management of multiple blogs and allows easy manipulation of embeds such as images, videos, and Markdown. Extensibility with plugins and themes provide easy ways to add functionality or change the looks of blog.

Robust content management

Flexible content management enables users to easily move through posts. Increase the usability of your blog by adding customized categories, sections, format, or flow. with this functionality, you're in full control.

State of the Art Infrastructure

With reliability and speed in mind, worldwide data centers provide the backbon for ultra-fast connectivity. This ensures your site will load instantly, no matter where your readers are, keeping your site competitive.

Free, open, simple

Blogr is a free and open source application backed by a large community of helpful developers. It supports fearures such as code syntax highlighting, RSS feeds, social media integration, third party commenting tools, and works seamlessly with Google Analytics. The architecture is clean and is relatively easy to learn.
